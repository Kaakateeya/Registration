Adding Photographs To Matrimonial Profiles

  1. 1.Why should I add my photograph to my matrimonial profile?

    Our statistics show that adding a photo to your matrimonial profile increases the number of times your profile is viewed by up to 7 times. You are likely to receive 12 times as many responses if you attach a photo. So we advise you to upload a photo to increase your chances of finding your partner quickly.

  2. 2.Is it safe to add my photos along with the profile?

    Yes, it is absolutely safe to add photos to your profile. Kaakateeya.com ensures that all photographs are watermarked so as to prevent your photos from being tampered with.

    Back to top

  3. 3.How do I add my photo to my matrimonial profile on Kaakateeya.com?

    To add your photo to your matrimonial profile please follow these steps.
    • Login to your My Kaakateeya Account.
    • Click on "Edit Photo" link available on the My Kaakateeya page and follow the instructions there.

    Back to top

  4. 4.Can I make any of my album photos my profile photo?

    Yes, you can swap any album photo with your profile photo.To do this, click on “Make Profile Photo” option in the dropdown menu for the album photo.

    Back to top

  5. 5.While uploading my photograph, I saw an error message that the image must be in jpg, gif, bmp, tiff or png format, what does this mean?

    jpg, gif, bmp, tiff and png are the most popular digital image formats on the internet. Kaakateeya.com accepts only these image formats for photos on your matrimonial profile.

    Back to top

  6. 6.How do I remove my photo from my matrimonial profile on Kaakateeya.com?

    To remove your photo on Kaakateeya.com all you need to do is:
    • Login to your My Kaakateeya Account.
    • Click on My Photos in My Profile available under My Kaakateeya on the left of your screen and follow the instructions there.
    • Click on the down arrow at the bottom right side of the photo graph you want to Delete
    • Choose Delete Photo

    Note: If you delete your profile photo then your first album photo will automatically become your profile photo.

  7. 7.Where are the photos I uploaded while registering?

    Kaakateeya.com reviews all photographs uploaded by members. While we allow members the ability to upload their photographs, we reserve the right to remove/delete any photograph that violates socially accepted norms of decency. It takes us up to 24 hours to screen your photos, during which period your photos will not be visible.

  8. 8.What privacy options are available for photos?

    Only Premium Members are entitled to the privacy options of 'Password Protection' and 'Photo Visible to Contacted and Accepted Members'. As a Premium Member, you can protect your photo with a password. In order to view your photograph other members will require the password. The other privacy option available is 'Visible on accept' which means, only those people who you have accepted or expressed interest in will be able to view your photo. You can change your privacy setting on the My Photos sectionunder My Kaakateeya.
